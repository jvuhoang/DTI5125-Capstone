What you can ask

The agent answers across seven clinical question types for any of the three diseases. Responses draw from both the ontology and PICOS-structured PubMed literature.

1 Symptoms "What are the early symptoms of Alzheimer's?"
2 Risk factors "Who is most at risk for Parkinson's disease?"
3 Treatment "What treatments are available for ALS?"
4 Diagnosis "How is Parkinson's disease diagnosed?"
5 Prognosis "What is the life expectancy with ALS?"
6 Clinical evidence "What clinical trials have studied levodopa outcomes?"
7 Differentiation "What distinguishes ALS from Parkinson's?"
PICOS-structured answers

Clinical literature answers are grounded in the PICOS framework — each response is traceable to specific populations, interventions, comparisons, outcomes, and study designs.

P
Population
Who was studied — disease, age group, sample size
I
Intervention
Treatment, drug, therapy, or exposure applied
C
Comparison
Control group or alternative treatment compared
O
Outcome
What was measured or observed in the study
S
Study design
RCT, cohort, case-control, systematic review
